"There is no passion in playing small."

--- Nelson Mandela

  • Intensely personal; it is all about YOU
  • Shining a torch on and dealing with your specific issues - your personal projects, your business success, general conditions and transitions taking place in your personal life, relationships or profession
  • Going deep into what is going on right now, discovering what personal obstacles or challenges you are facing
  • Unearthing areas where you are 'playing small'
  • Conscious and empowered choices to make your life what you want it to be
  • Different from consulting, mentoring, advice, therapy, or counseling (although it can work in conjunction with other therapies). In life coaching, you are the expert of your life. You know who you are and what you need. Your Chrysalis lifecoach is trained to work with functioning clients and will not treat or diagnose but will uncover your "best" and move you to a more balanced and more fulfilling life
  • The official definition given by the International Coaching Federation ('ICF') is "partnering with clients in a thought provoking and creative process that inspires them to maximise their personal and professional potential"

Coaching with Chrysalis Lifecoach

    Individual Life Coaching - How does it work

  • For individual life coaching sessions, please contact us. Send an email dubai.chrysalis@gmail.com or call +97155 972 6175
  • You will receive a proposal and additional information on life coaching with Chrysalis Lifecoach.
  • We will arrange a convenient meeting place.
  • You can choose to have only one session if desired; however for you to benefit most a minimum of 10 sessions is recommended.
  • After every coaching session, you will have an assignment to do

    Structure

  • An initial discovery session - "Design the Alliance" first session which lasts around 2 hours
  • One 30 to 60 minute coaching session (in person or on the phone) once a week or bi-monthly
  • Unlimited email support
  • Occasional brief check-in calls
